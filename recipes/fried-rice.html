Steaming Fried Rice

Fried rice is a beloved dish originating from Chinese cuisine, known for its savory flavor and versatility. Made by stir-frying cooked rice with a mix of ingredients like vegetables, proteins (such as chicken, shrimp, or tofu), and seasoned with soy sauce, garlic, and ginger, it transforms leftovers into a satisfying meal.

Celebrated for its adaptability, fried rice can be customized to suit various tastes and dietary preferences. Often garnished with green onions or sesame seeds, itâ€™s a convenient and delicious option, whether served as a main course or a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old for best texture)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil (or sesame oil)
  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2-3 green onions, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce (or to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Protein of choice (chicken, shrimp, or tofu) - optional
